02 — What we focus on

Our Manifesto

Eight pillars that guide the 48th Administration’s commitment to every Poly Ibadan student.

Core pillars

Manifesto Pillars

📚

Academic Welfare

Advocating for fair grading, accessible lecturers, well-stocked libraries and timely release of results.

🛡

Security & Safety

Working with management and campus security to keep hostels, lecture halls and walkways safe day and night.

⚽

Sports & Recreation

Organising inter-departmental games, supporting sporting talents and improving recreation facilities.

❤

Welfare & Health

Improving access to the clinic, subsidised welfare packages, and support for students in financial difficulty.

📣

Advocacy & Representation

Speaking up for students on tuition, accommodation and policy matters before the school's management.

🎉

Events & Engagement

Hosting orientation, cultural week, award nights and events that bring students together.

💡

Innovation & Technology

Leveraging technology to improve student services, communication and access to information.

🌿

Environment & Sustainability

Promoting a clean, green and safe campus environment for all students.

Our Pledge

“We pledge to serve with integrity, transparency and dedication — putting students first in every decision.”

— Fagbemi Nurudeen Ojo, SUG President, 48th Administration
